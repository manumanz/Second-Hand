SECOND HAND

you are the inside of a stranger's coat pocket
stranger no.  ·  or summon one:
seven days. you never see them — only what falls in, and how they move.
how to play

you are the inside of a stranger's coat pocket, for seven days. you never see them — when the pocket sways, they're walking. when everything gets squashed, they sat down. when it all goes still and blue, it's night.

watch what falls in. every object is a clue about who they are — and one of them matters more than all the rest.

drag to push things around. you are weak while they move and strong while they rest. wait for sitting, or night.

click a thing to examine it. it gets written into the pocket book (top right): what it is, and what it tells you about them.

examine again at night. in the blue light, every object gives up one extra secret. the pocket book keeps those too.

the mystery: the pocket book shows four possible people. only one is really them. use your clues to rule people out — at the end of the week, you pick your answer.

the hole (lower right) pulls in anything small that gets close — and it grows every day. drag things to safety, or feed it something they'd be better off losing. rub across the hole to stitch it tighter for a while.

the fingers dive in hunting for things. leave the thing where they can find it — or hide it in the dark corner, low on the left, where they never search. both choices change the story.

at the end of the week the coat is given away. everything you kept becomes someone else's lucky find.
